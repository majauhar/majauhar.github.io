Research

Exploring AI safety, multi-agent systems, and machine learning

My research focuses on developing safe, aligned, and capable AI systems. I'm particularly interested in multi-agent systems, reinforcement learning, and AI safety techniques that ensure beneficial outcomes as AI capabilities advance.

Research Interests

  • AI Safety and Alignment
  • Multi-Agent Systems
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Long-horizon Task Evaluation
  • Capability and Safety Benchmarking

Current Projects

Evaluating AI Agent Capabilities on Long-horizon Tasks

Ongoing 2024-Present

Developing evaluation frameworks to assess AI agent performance and safety alignment in complex, multi-step tasks that require planning and reasoning over extended time horizons.

Multi-Agent Systems with CAMEL-AI

Ongoing 2024-Present

Exploring communication and collaboration mechanisms in multi-agent environments using the CAMEL-AI framework, focusing on emergent behaviors and system robustness.
