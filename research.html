Research

Exploring Alignment, multi-agent systems, and fundamental problems in machine learning

My research focuses on developing safe, aligned, and capable AI systems. I'm particularly interested LLM/agent evaluation, multi-agent system, and post-training techniques for reasoning and alignment that ensure beneficial outcomes as AI capabilities advances.

Current Research Interests

AI Alignment • Multi-Agent Systems • Long horizon/context Task Evaluation • Generalisation

Current Projects

Evaluating AI Agent Capabilities and Alignment on Long-horizon Tasks

Ongoing July 2025-Present

Developing evaluation frameworks to assess AI agent performance and safety alignment in long-context or multi-step tasks that require planning and reasoning over extended time horizons.

Multi-Agent Systems with CAMEL-AI

Ongoing March 2025-Present

Exploring communication and collaboration mechanisms in multi-agent environments using the CAMEL-AI framework and Eigent, focusing on system robustness and real-world applications.
